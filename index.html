How do vehicle characteristics correlate to model Sales, Re-Sale Price, Sale Price?

This data visualization attempts to chart the correlation between different vehicle characteristics and the vehicle's sale price, re-sale price and overall sales.

The charts are grouped by characteristic types,

  1. Engine_size
  2. Horsepower
  3. Wheelbase
  4. Width
  5. Length
  6. Curb_weight
  7. Fuel_capacity
  8. Fuel_efficiency
  9. Power_perf_factor

How to use this visualization
  • The y-axis changes according to the selctions
  • The x-axis is specific to each chart's vehicle characteristic.
  • The annotation indicates the correlation in the chart
